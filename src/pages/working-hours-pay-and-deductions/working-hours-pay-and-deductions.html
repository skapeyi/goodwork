Pay, Deductions and Overtime What does the law say?
  1. You have a right to the wages specified in your service contract. (The Service Contract is often referred to as the Ministry of Labour Contract).
  2. You have a right to receive your salary yourself and to be paid at least once every month.
  3. You have a right to be paid for any additional hours you work above your regular working hours (i.e. overtime).
  4. You have a right to receive your salary in your bank account, under the Wage Protection System.
  5. You have a right to all unpaid dues from your employer before you exit Qatar on annual leave.
 What can I do if there is a problem with my pay, deductions or overtime?
  1. Payments below contractual agreement
  2. Prolonged training / probationary period *
  3. Not paid into worker's personal account
  4. Lack of renumeration for injury/ sickness due to poor working or living conditions
  5. Excess/ previously unagreed charges for training, equipment, uniform, living/ travel expenses
